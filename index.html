SCP 1537: Balloonlum

About Balloonlum

SCP 1537, also know as Balloonlum is believed to be a species that desguises itself as a floating balloon inorder to lure people in so that it can devourer them. They seem to take the apperance of there victims afterward, we are unsure if this is just an apperance or if the victim is still alive inside as they seem to be unresponsive. So far there is only one reported incident of this happening and we are unsure of what this SCP wants as it seems to now be passive after devouring it's victim.

The above image is of the first and only recorded victim of SCP 1537.

Strengths

  • Can fly
  • Bullet proof
  • Can appear as a normal balloon at will

Weaknesses

  • Seems to want to carry on with the life of it's victim
  • Doesn't seem to be able to communicate
